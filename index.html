About Us

Vestar College was established in 1950 with the vision to foster excellence in education and research. Accredited by top educational bodies, our mission is to nurture talent, encourage innovation, and contribute to societal development.

Academic Programs

  • Undergraduate: B.Sc, B.A, B.Com, B.Tech
  • Postgraduate: M.Sc, M.A, M.Com, M.Tech
  • Research: Ph.D. in Sciences, Arts, Commerce, and Engineering
  • Departments: Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science, English, Business, Engineering

Admissions

We welcome applications from eligible candidates every year. Please review the admission procedures, eligibility criteria, and important dates below.

  • Admission Procedure: Online application via nasccbe.ac.in
  • Eligibility: Minimum 50% in qualifying exams
  • Application Deadline: August 31
  • Entrance Exams: Required for some programs
